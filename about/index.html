About Minkyu Choi


Intro

Minkyu Choi is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) research scientist and engineer with over five years of experience across various industries. Currently pursuing a Ph.D. at the University of Texas at Austin under Dr. Sandeep Chinchali‘s supervision. His research interests include Deep Reinforcement Learning, Neuro-Symbolic AI, Video Understanding, and Computer Vision Assurance. He possesses expertise in Data Engineering, Cloud and Edge Computing, DevSecOps, and MLOps, and has worked in commercial and government sectors on machine learning and deep learning projects.
